Assalamu Alaikum!
Welcome to Ali Shaihani Group of Industries, a leading brand entity in Oman’s food and beverage sector. We have, with our home-grown ‘Chips Oman’, and a diverse range of food products, become a household name in Oman and the region. We owe this success to you, our dear patron, who has stood by us, loyally, since we began our operations more than four decades ago. Your trust in our expertise and confidence in our quality products continue to propel us to greater heights.

What began as a marketing unit for soft drinks and consumer items has grown into a manufacturing powerhouse with our ‘Made in Oman’ growth tenet, which has established our credentials as a local company with global recognition. From its very inception, we have followed a growth strategy focused on delivering what we promise, with quality products that are on par with international standards and have that unique taste factor that makes them truly Omani. Today, we are an establishment of national and international merit, and we intend to seek further glory in shaping Oman’s developmental goals, as crafted in the ambitious ‘Oman Vision 2040.’ While the customer has and will continue to remain the focal point of our future development plans, our continued progress in our day-to-day operations solely lies in the dedication our team of employees brings to the table. They belong to the ever-growing Ali Shaihani family and are equally committed to ensuring success in our every venture. It is thanks to them that we today boast a prolific marketing and distribution network that has helped in transforming the Ali Shaihani Group of Industries into an enviable organization.

As pioneers in the industry, the onus is on us to create and develop unique product propositions for our customers and to engage with them through our diversified product range. We will continue to be the market leaders with our innovations, our sustainable business practices, and our established hallmark of excellence.

DIRECTOR’S MESSAGE

It gives me great pleasure to welcome you all to our website. You have been a part of the Ali Shaihani Group of Industries as loyal patrons and associates, and this new portal will demonstrate our gratitude by connecting you to our growing enterprise. Our four major business units – Ali Shaihani Food Industries LLC, Ali Shaihani Soft Drinks Industries LLC, Al Jufair Food Industry LLC, and Al Shaihani Paper Industries LLC – have penetrated the local and regional markets to shape our fortunes as market leaders; our product range is a validation of our credentials as a local company with a global profile.

We have exemplified our thought leadership in business with market studies and constant assessment of changing consumer needs to upgrade our products and forge newer avenues for growth. We have also underscored our intent to tap Oman’s human resources and shape the future of our young workforce by honing their skills through training facilities and on-the-job coaching. We acknowledge their importance in establishing our footprints in the country and the region; their personal growth within the organisation is a reflection of our tenacity to build the national workforce and be one with the Government in achieving the objectives of ‘Oman Vision 2040’.
